# About Airdrop Hunter

## What is Airdrop Hunter?

Airdrop Hunter is an automated intelligence tool for Web3 crypto enthusiasts who want to stay on top of airdrop opportunities without spending hours on research.

## The Problem

- **Information Overload**: Hundreds of airdrop opportunities flood Twitter, Discord, and Telegram daily
- **Time-Consuming Research**: Manually checking 10+ sources takes 2+ hours daily
- **Scam Risk**: Phishing sites and rug pulls are common
- **Missed Opportunities**: High-value airdrops often have short windows

## The Solution

Airdrop Hunter automates the entire research process:

1. **Scans** multiple sources (Twitter, Galxe, Mirror, research platforms)
2. **Filters** out expired and low-value opportunities
3. **Grades** projects by risk/reward (S/A/B system)
4. **Delivers** a concise daily action checklist

## Who Should Use This?

- **Airdrop Hunters** who want to maximize efficiency
- **Crypto Newcomers** who don't know where to start
- **Busy Professionals** who can't spend hours on research
- **Risk-Averse Users** who need security warnings

## Key Differentiators

| Feature | Airdrop Hunter | Manual Research |
|---------|----------------|-----------------|
| Time Required | 5 min/day | 2+ hours/day |
| Risk Assessment | Automated grading | Manual evaluation |
| Security Alerts | Built-in warnings | Must verify yourself |
| Missed Deadlines | Automated reminders | Easy to miss |

## How It Works

```
Search Plugins → Multi-source scanning
       ↓
   AI Processing → Filter, grade, format
       ↓
   Daily Report → Deliver to chat/Telegram
```

## Grading Methodology

### Grade S (Must Do)
- Tier-1 VC backing (a16z, Paradigm, Polychain)
- $50M+ funding
- Mainnet live with points system
- Low cost (<$5)

### Grade A (High Priority)
- Reputable VCs
- $10M+ funding
- Confirmed token or imminent TGE
- Reasonable cost ($5-20)

### Grade B (Speculative)
- Testnet stage
- Zero-cost interaction
- Early-stage potential
- No guarantees

## Security Features

- **Domain Verification**: Cross-check with official Twitter
- **Phishing Detection**: Flag similar domain names
- **Contract Verification**: Check on block explorers
- **Community Reports**: Search for scam warnings

## Technology Stack

- **Platform**: Coze AI
- **Plugins**: Google Search, Link Reader, Twitter Search
- **Workflow**: Scheduled automation for daily delivery
- **Output**: Chat, Telegram, or Discord
